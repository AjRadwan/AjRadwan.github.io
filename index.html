About Me

Hey there! I'm Radwan

A former software engineer turned copywriter. By the Way this website is built by me.

My love for coding started at the age of 16 when I first started tinkering with lines of code, and it quickly became a major part of my life. I spent 5 years typing lines of code and figuring out how to make computers do what I want them to do. But let's be honest, the computer never laughed at my jokes.

Soon, I realized that writing words that can change people’s mind and life is more fun than staring at a screen all day. So I decide to retire as a Young Engineer. And pursue copywriting and work with something a little more human.

Nowadays, I spend my days crafting persuasive copy that grabs people's attention and gets them hooked. And let me tell you, it's a lot more fun than debugging code.

I’m also working on building my one person little business. I don’t level myself as an entrepreneur, but I do have a few digital products that tackle real-world problems. My ultimate goal is simple: make one life better every day. It may sound cheesy, but hey, isn't that what life is all about?

I believe that every single one of us has the power to make a positive impact on someone's life, no matter how big or small. Whether it's through my digital products or just a simple act of kindness, I strive to make a difference in this world. I'm proud of what I do and the impact I make. After all, isn't it better to focus on the value you bring rather than just a title?
